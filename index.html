How RMA Blaster Works

  • Shared database: entries and PDFs live in the cloud — everyone signed in sees the same data instantly
  • Logins: each team member has their own email + password; accounts are created by the administrator
  • Sessions: you stay signed in until you sign out — no weekly re-login
  • PDFs: open an entry → tap Download next to each file
  • Excel: click Download Excel on the dashboard; on iPad files land in the Files app
